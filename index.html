Monitor Your Robot Experiments

Train batches of models, deploy them to your robot, and Xpy will live-plot the results. Your experiments no longer need to be a black box.

Organize your experiments by model, dataset, and more. Xpy allows you to group your experiments however you want and tag them according to your needs.

Generate custom reports to compare data across experiments. Xpy manages your logged robot data across runs so you can focus on building better models.
